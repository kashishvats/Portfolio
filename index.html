What I Do

Science that converts.
Copy that doesn't lie.

Most skincare brands sit on genuinely good science — well-researched ingredients, thoughtful formulations, real mechanisms of action. But that science either stays buried in an INCI list, or gets flattened into vague marketing claims that consumers no longer trust.

I sit at the intersection of the lab and the page. With an MSc in Organic Chemistry and a theoretical grounding in cosmetic formulation science, I understand what your ingredients actually do — and I write about them in a way that is accurate, layered, and genuinely persuasive. No exaggeration. No miracle claims. Just the science, told well.

01
Ingredient Science Articles

Long-form pieces that trace the biological mechanism, historical use, and modern evidence behind your hero ingredients. Written to educate your consumer and position your brand as the most scientifically credible voice in the room.

02
Product Science Narratives

The full story of a product — why these ingredients, why this combination, why it works the way it does. Ideal for launch content, PDFs, and brand decks where the formulation itself needs to be the centrepiece.

03
Formulation Storytelling

I take your R&D brief — the pH decisions, the emulsifier rationale, the actives — and turn it into content your consumer actually wants to read. This is where my chemistry background matters most.

04
Brand Ghostwriting

You have the science. I write the words, in your voice, under your name. Whether it's founder content, educational series, or product copy, I make sure the chemistry behind your brand is never left unexplained.

05
Scientific Accuracy Editing

Already have copy, but unsure if the science holds up? I review and edit skincare content for factual accuracy, appropriate claims, and scientific integrity. No overclaiming, no underselling.

06
Ancient-to-Modern Bridging

A speciality: connecting traditional Ayurvedic or botanical ingredients to their modern biochemical evidence. Particularly powerful for heritage brands who want to speak to today's evidence-driven consumer.
